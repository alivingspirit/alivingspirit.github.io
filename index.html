This is the temporary registration site for the Hebrew Theological College Alumni Basketball League for the 2015-2016 season.

If you haven't played in the league before, please read the info below carefully.

ABL League Basics

There are 16 teams, each consisting of 7 players, and each led by a captain.

There is a draft held before the beginning of each season with 6 rounds.

The captain who drafts you will notify you of the name of your team so you can check the calendar posted on our website for your game times each week.

Each team plays 14 games and gets two bye weeks.

Schedule

The first games are tentatively scheduled for Sunday, November 1st.

Games are almost every Sunday night for approximately 16 weeks with a 2-3 week playoff round to determine a champion. 

Games are played at either Hillel Torah or Fairview Middle School in Skokie. 

Most games are scheduled at 8 and 9 o'clock with one 10 o'clock game each week.

If you would like to play, but know that there are a number of Sunday nights that you will not be available, please email the commissioner at the email address below, because captains need to know this information before the draft.

Registration

The fee to play is $160.00.

Proceeds go to pay for jerseys, to rent locations, to pay professional referees, and other expenses.

Extra funds at the end of the year go to charity.

To Register:

  1. Pay with PayPal.
  2. Send a picture of yourself to commissioner@yeshivaabl.com
  3. Fill out the form with your contact information and height.

There are a few captain spots open.  If you are interested in being a captain, please email the commissioner.

For all questions email: commissioner@yeshivaabl.com

Thank you and have an enjoyable year.
